Study Rationale – How to Think (Not Memorize)

Use this section for trainer self-study. Do NOT share with learners.

  • Delay Errors: Any option that waits, reassesses, documents, or monitors before safety action is unsafe.
  • Scope Errors: If a nurse can act without an order, waiting for MD is unsafe.
  • ESL Traps: Grammatically impressive or complex wording is often wrong.
  • Trainer Drift: High scores + slow time = coaching, not readiness.
  • Rationale Rule: One sentence. Action-focused. No language analysis.
  • Release Rule: Dashboard logic overrides trainer opinion.
